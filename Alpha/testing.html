Journey

July 9, 2022

I'm starting this website and blog now because I want to create a portfolio. Although I have been studying Python on and off for the past 2 years, I do not feel competent enough to actually apply for a job. The hospital where I work has nursing informatics positions open that require Python, SQL, and Tableau. I haven't started with Tableau yet, but will probably get around to it within the next year or two. For the time being, I will focus on creating Python projects and developing a website. The plan is to have something up and running by the time I reach my 2 year mark so that I can apply for nursing informatics positions internally.

My first goal is to create a functional navbar, but I'm also playing around with Figma online to try and make a wireframe. I'd like to get the wireframe first, but the website requires an artistic touch that I just don't have. Instead, I'm going to work on them simultaneously. Perhaps I can work on the index page piece by piece, but I'll eventually make individual, unique pages once I figure out Figma.
